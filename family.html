Srinagar

take shikara rides on Dal Lake, stroll through Mughal gardens, and enjoy snow fun in Gulmarg." , "Shikara Ride, Mughal Gardens, Dal Lake

Srinagar (Shikara Ride, Mughal Gardens, Dal Lake, Gulmarg Snow Fun)

Duration: 4N / 5D

Day 1: Arrival at Srinagar → Check-in at hotel/houseboat → Evening Shikara ride on Dal Lake → Overnight in Srinagar.

Day 2: Visit Mughal Gardens (Shalimar, Nishat, Chashme Shahi) → Hazratbal Shrine → Leisure at Boulevard Road → Overnight in Srinagar.

Day 3: Excursion to Gulmarg → Enjoy Gondola ride, skiing/snow activities → Return to Srinagar → Overnight stay.

Day 4: Full-day trip to Pahalgam → Explore Betaab Valley & Lidder River views → Evening return to Srinagar → Overnight stay.

Day 5: Morning free for local bazaar shopping (handicrafts, dry fruits, pashmina) → Departure from Srinagar Airport.

Darjeeling

Known for its toy train, tea gardens, and sunrise at Tiger Hill – Darjeeling offers something delightful for kids, parents, and grandparents alike.

Toy Train, Tiger Hill sunrise

Darjeeling (Toy Train, Tea Gardens, Tiger Hill Sunrise)

Duration: 4N / 5D

Day 1: Arrival at NJP Railway Station / Bagdogra Airport → Drive to Darjeeling → Check-in at hotel → Evening leisure walk at Mall Road → Overnight in Darjeeling.

Day 2: Early morning visit to Tiger Hill for sunrise over Kanchenjunga → On return, stop at Ghoom Monastery & Batasia Loop → Breakfast → Visit Himalayan Mountaineering Institute, Zoo & Japanese Peace Pagoda → Overnight stay.

Day 3: Ride the famous Darjeeling Toy Train (UNESCO Heritage) → Visit Tea Gardens & factory tour → Explore local markets → Overnight in Darjeeling.

Day 4: Excursion to Mirik Lake or Kalimpong (optional) → Enjoy boating, horse rides, and valley views → Return to Darjeeling → Overnight stay.

Day 5: Morning free for shopping (tea, handicrafts) → Check-out → Transfer to NJP/Bagdogra for departure. markets → Airport drop.

Gangtok

A safe and serene hill town where families can explore monasteries, enjoy ropeway rides, and experience the thrill of Nathula Pass.

Changu Lake, Baba Mandir

Gangtok (Monasteries, Ropeway, Nathula Pass Thrill)

Duration: 4N / 5D

Day 1: Arrival at NJP Railway Station / Bagdogra Airport → Drive to Gangtok → Check-in at hotel → Evening leisure walk at MG Marg → Overnight in Gangtok.

Day 2: Full-day Gangtok city tour → Visit Rumtek Monastery, Enchey Monastery & Do Drul Chorten → Ropeway ride over the city → Flower Exhibition Centre → Overnight stay.

Day 3: Excursion to Tsomgo Lake & Baba Mandir → Enjoy yak rides and snow play (seasonal) → Return to Gangtok → Overnight stay.

Day 4: Day trip to Nathula Pass (Indo-China border, subject to permits & weather) → Experience high-altitude views → Return to Gangtok → Evening shopping at local markets → Overnight stay.

Day 5: Morning free for local souvenirs → Departure to NJP/Bagdogra for onward journey. markets → Airport drop.

Kerala

enjoy peaceful backwater houseboat rides, lush tea gardens, and calm lagoons that everyone can cherish together.

Houseboat stay in Alleppey/Kumarakom

Kerala (Backwaters, Tea Gardens, Calm Lagoons)

Duration: 4N / 5D

Day 1: Arrival at Kochi Airport → Drive to Munnar → Check-in at hotel → Evening free at leisure → Overnight in Munnar.

Day 2: Full-day sightseeing in Munnar → Visit Tea Gardens & Tea Museum → Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point & Kundala Lake → Evening stroll through local market → Overnight in Munnar.

Day 3: Drive to Thekkady → Explore Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary with boat ride → Spice plantation tour → Optional Kathakali show / martial arts → Overnight in Thekkady.

Day 4: Check-in to traditional houseboat → Cruise through backwaters & lagoons → Enjoy meals on board → Overnight stay in houseboat.

Day 5: Drive to Kochi → Short city tour (Fort Kochi, Chinese Fishing Nets, Mattancherry) → Departure from Kochi Airport
