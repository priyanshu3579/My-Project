Adventure Destinations

Thrill, fun and unforgettable journeys await you!

Top Adventure Destinations

Andaman Islands

Pristine beaches and thrilling water sports await you.

Water sports, Scuba, Snorkeling, Ross Island boat rides

Andaman (Water sports, scuba diving, snorkeling, Ross Island boat rides)

Duration: 4N / 5D

Day 1: Arrival at Port Blair → Check-in → Evening at Corbyn’s Cove Beach → Light & Sound Show at Cellular Jail → Overnight at Port Blair.

Day 2: Excursion to Ross Island & North Bay → Enjoy boat rides, snorkeling, and coral views → Return to Port Blair.

Day 3: Ferry to Havelock Island → Visit Radhanagar Beach → Overnight stay at Havelock.

Day 4: Optional water sports at Elephant Beach → Later transfer to Neil Island → Overnight stay.

Day 5: Return to Port Blair → Shopping at local markets → Airport drop.

Gulmarg

Skiing, snow adventures and breathtaking landscapes.

Gondola ride, skiing, snow adventure

Gulmarg (Gondola ride, skiing, snow adventure)

Duration: 2N / 3D

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar → Drive to Gulmarg → Evening walk in meadows → Overnight stay in hotel.

Day 2: Enjoy Gondola Ride (Phase 1 & Phase 2) → Skiing / Snowboarding activities → Visit Maharani Temple & St. Mary’s Church → Overnight in Gulmarg.

Day 3: Leisure time → Return to Srinagar for onward journey.

Pahalgam

Nature trails, river rafting, and serene valleys.

Aru Valley, Betaab Valley trekking, river rafting

Pahalgam (Aru Valley, Betaab Valley trekking, river rafting)

Duration: 2N / 3D

Day 1: Arrive in Srinagar → Transfer to Pahalgam → Check-in → Evening walk along Lidder River → Overnight stay.

Day 2: Day trip to Aru Valley & Betaab Valley (trekking, scenic views) → Optional river rafting in Lidder River → Overnight stay in Pahalgam.

Day 3: Explore local saffron fields & markets → Return to Srinagar.

Sonmarg

Snow-capped mountains and adventurous treks.

Thajiwas Glacier trek, snow activities

Sonmarg (Thajiwas Glacier trek, snow activities)

Duration: 1N / 2D

Day 1: Drive from Srinagar to Sonmarg (approx 80 km) → Local sightseeing (river valley views, fishing spots) → Pony ride to Thajiwas Glacier → Snow fun activities → Overnight stay in Sonmarg.

Day 2: Morning leisure walk by Sindh River → Local market visit → Return to Srinagar.
