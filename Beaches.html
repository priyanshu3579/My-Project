Andaman Islands

Crystal clear waters, soft sandy beaches, and safe family activities like snorkeling and boat rides make Andaman a tropical paradise for all ages.

Havelock Island, Radhanagar Beach, Elephant Beach, Neil Island

Havelock Island, Radhanagar Beach, Elephant Beach, Neil Island

Duration: 4N / 5D

Day 1: Arrival at Port Blair → Check-in → Evening at Corbyn’s Cove Beach → Overnight stay.

Day 2: Ferry to Havelock Island → Visit Radhanagar Beach → Optional snorkeling / water sports → Overnight stay.

Day 3: Elephant Beach activities (banana boat, scuba, snorkeling) → Overnight stay at Havelock.

Day 4: Neil Island sightseeing → Relax on beaches → Overnight stay.

Day 5: Return to Port Blair → Local market shopping → Airport drop.

Kerala

From Kovalam to Varkala, Kerala’s beaches are calm, clean, and perfect for families to relax, play by the waves, and enjoy beautiful sunsets together.

Kovalam Beach

Kovalam Beach

Duration: 3N / 4D

Day 1: Arrival in Trivandrum → Transfer to Kovalam → Check-in → Beachside leisure → Overnight stay.

Day 2: Full day beach activities, lighthouse visit, local seafood experience → Overnight stay.

Day 3: Optional day trip to Kanyakumari / backwaters → Overnight stay.

Day 4: Checkout → Airport / station drop.
